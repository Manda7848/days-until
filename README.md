# days-until!(A.K.A Freedom 1.0)

This project is very dear to my heart becaue it serves as a reminder of the coming milestone in my life. The end of high school.

It came as an idea to me when my classmates and I wanted to have a countdown on the board so we rote it on notes, took a screenshot then set that as the wallpaper. 
That was innefective because it:
-had weird cropping issues
-was really hard to calculate the days every morning while people who didn't care just wanted to watch youtube
-was ugly

Thanks to Athena Sleepover and Hack Club, I got the idea to solve that issue using a website that could work offline.

Then I hit a hurdle, the default font for HTML files with no CSS on chrome is Times New Roman and that would have ruined the page. I embarked on a search to find a pre-installed font on windows that looked good(come to think of it, I could have techically just downloaded a typeface from the internet and put it in the folder).

The design also has a back story because when I took coding classes, our first website was about someone and we used a CSS card with a box shadow and border radius and it looked so cool and clean. I had also been experimenting with borders earlier. The light blue colour was a nice touch since our uniform is blue.

My classmates and teachers see it everyday and the days are just flowing by, when I coded it, it was at 70 days and now, just about 40 remain.

High School was fun as I reflect on it now.
Alot of ups and downs, many situations that I will look back and cringe about, wonderful conversations and debates, and most of all, my amazing friends.
